Eggs and Bacon

  • Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4 Medium or large whole eggs
Bacon 3 strips of your favroite bacon
cheese 1 cup of mexican blend cheese
salt 2-3 pinches of salt
Pepper 1 pinch of black pepper
oil 2 teaspoon

Step 1: Wash your pan, utensils and cooking area.

Step 2: Heat up your 1st pan to medium heat.

Step 3: Place 3 strips of your favorice bacon on your pan

Step 4: let the bacon sit on your pan for 2-5 minutes or untill maple brown or darker depending on your preference.

Step 5: Oil your 2nd pan with 2 tsp .

Step 6: crack three eggs into a bowl, and break up the yolk with a wisk or fork untill the eggs are in a yellow liquid form. You can also use the egg whites and save the egg yolks for someone else if you are cutting calories.

Step 7: Pour your eggs into your pan once your pan is hot.

Step 8: Turn your pan stove heat down a bit so the eggs can cook evenly and not burn.

Step 9: Pour your eggs into your pan once your pan is hot.

Step 10:once your eggs start to take on a solid form, add your salt and pepper to add falvor.

Step 11: Check on your bacon. If your bacon has a light brown color it is time to turn your bacon over. If both sides are medium or dark brown than your bacon is done.

step 12: It's time to check on your eggs or remove your eggs from the hot stove or hot pan and add your cheese!

step 13: Are your eggs a solid yellow or white color? Is your bacon dark or medium brown? Have you added cheese , salt & pepper? If you have great!! Throw those bad boy's on a plate and enjoy your breakfast!

Hamburger recipe

  • Time: 15mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Groud Beef 1/2 lbs
chopped onion 1
Salt 1tsp
Pepper 1 tsp
Chedder cheese 4 thin cut slices
tomato 1 tomato cut into 4 equally thick cut slices
ingredient qty
Lettuce 3-4 medium to large leaves

1. Make sure your cooking utensils and surfaces are clean.

2.Cut your Tomato into four thick and equal width slices

3.Cut your onion in half, Dice one half into small but equal squares.

4.Cut the other half of your onion into equally sized thick slices.

5.Mix your diced onions into your ground beef.

6. shape your beef mixed with onion into a circular patties shape.

7.heat up your pan or grill

8.place your patties on to your heated surface and set a timer to 8 minutes. if on a stove you can use the number 8 setting.

9. at 8 minutes turn your pattie over

10.Cook the other side of your pattie for 6 minutes.

11. Place your sliced cheese on your pattie and let it sit on the pattie for the remaining 2 minutes.

12. Grill your remaining patties, onions and tomatoes for about 20-40 seconds and place them on your cooked hamburger pattie.

13. cover the flat surface of your buns with a thin layer of butter and place them on your pan for 20-40 seconds.

14. ENJOY YOUR BURGER!!!
